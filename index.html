PenOpus is an online prescription platform for all Doctors. The platform combines cutting edge responsive design to enable composing prescription of your choice. PenOpus is borne out of the compelling need of penning prescriptions that are easy to read, understand and help patients to follow the advised dosing. PenOpus gives you complete control from the look and feel of your prescription to penning the history, medicines and follow up advise – all of this accessible from the choicest of your devices like your PC, Mobile, Tab.

Evolution of good old paper-based prescription to PenOpus Prescription is in the making…
